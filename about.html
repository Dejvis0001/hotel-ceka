Hotel Ceka
Enjoy A Wonderfull Experience

Hotel Ceka offers accommodation with free WiFi and free private parking. The property is located 600 m from La Petite Beach, 800 m from Maestral Beach and 17 km from Butrint National Park. The hotel has family rooms. The nearest airport is Ioannina Airport, 99 km from the hotel. This is our guests’ favourite part of Sarandë, according to independent reviews. Couples particularly like the location — they rated it 9.0 for a two-person trip
